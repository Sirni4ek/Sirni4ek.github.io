Ecology in Sweden

Sweden is well-known for its strong commitment to protecting the environment. The country has made great efforts to use renewable energy sources, like wind and water, to reduce its impact on the planet.

Recycling is also very important in Sweden. People recycle a lot of their waste, and there are many programs to help reduce trash and reuse materials.

Sweden's forests and wildlife are protected by strict laws. There are many national parks and nature reserves where animals and plants are safe from harm.
